About Fitzroy

Founded in 2010, Fitzroy Accounting emerged from a vision to revolutionize how businesses approach their financial management. Our founders, experienced CPAs with decades of combined experience, recognized the need for a more proactive and technology-driven approach to accounting services that would help businesses not just survive, but thrive in the modern economy.

Our mission is to empower businesses through financial clarity, strategic insights, and innovative solutions. We believe in building lasting partnerships with our clients, founded on trust, transparency, and a shared commitment to excellence. Our core values of integrity, innovation, and client success drive everything we do, ensuring we deliver exceptional service that goes beyond traditional accounting.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer for small businesses?

We provide comprehensive accounting services including bookkeeping, tax planning, financial forecasting, and business advisory services tailored to small business needs. Our solutions scale with your growth.

How often will I receive financial reports?

We provide monthly financial reports as standard, with real-time access to key metrics through our digital platform. Custom reporting schedules are available based on your needs.

What makes your audit preparation service unique?

Our audit preparation service includes year-round monitoring, proactive issue resolution, and comprehensive documentation preparation. We ensure you're always audit-ready, not just at year-end.

How do you handle data security?

We employ bank-level encryption, secure cloud storage, and regular security audits. Our systems comply with all major regulatory requirements including GDPR and SOC 2.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing models based on service scope and business size. Our packages start at $500/month for basic services, with custom solutions available for more complex needs.
